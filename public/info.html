About Us

We started our business in Braselton, GA back in 2005, and we have been operating the business for over 15 years since. Our goal is to satisfy all customers' requests, and to do this we offer the best service available. Aside from our profession in drycleaning and laundering, we also offer services in wedding dresses, swedes & leather, comforters, mattresses, shoes, bags, and many more. Our seamtress is rich in experience, and will be able to provide a satisfactory alterations service. As of now, Our stores in Hoschton and Bethlehem are providing services in local area. And, we also provide delivery service. We will do our best to fulfil the needs of our customers.

Delivery Process

  • 1. Click "Delivery" tab to navigate to delivery section

  • 4. When your clothes are ready, we will then return your clothes to your front door after designated time
